Meet Kena D. Scott

Why I Coach: Renewing Strength & Spirit

Hi, I'm Kena. For years, I struggled to find balance. I fell into the trap of extreme dieting and severe physical and mental burnout, trying to meet unrealistic standards while neglecting my spiritual well-being.

It wasn't until I aligned my health journey with my faith—renewing my mind and treating my body as a temple of God—that everything changed. I broke the cycle of burnout and found sustainable, grace-filled strength. That is why I coach.

My mission is to help you bridge the gap between where you are today and where God is calling you to be. Lasting transformation isn't built on extreme restriction; it is built on consistent, intentional daily habits, functional movement, and spiritual grounding. Together, we'll build strength, confidence, and discipline that extends beyond physical fitness into every area of your life.
