Meet Kena D. Scott

A Holistic Approach to Strength & Growth

Hi, I'm Kena. My mission is to help you bridge the gap between where you are today and where God is calling you to be in your health and wellness journey. I believe lasting transformation happens through consistency, wisdom, and grace—not through quick fixes, fad diets, or extreme restrictions.

My coaching approach is rooted in biblical principles that encourage us to honor our bodies as temples, renew our minds, and steward our health with purpose. Through functional movement, sustainable nutrition habits, mindset coaching, and intentional daily choices, I help individuals create healthy lifestyles that support lasting change. Together, we'll build strength, confidence, discipline, and wellness that extends beyond physical fitness into every area of life. This is more than a fitness program—it's a journey toward becoming healthier in body, mind, and spirit.
